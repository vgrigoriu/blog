## Discovering Baby Goats in Piatra Neamț

Piatra Neamț, a charming city nestled in the Eastern Carpathians of Romania, is known for its stunning mountain views and rich cultural heritage. But one of the most delightful surprises you'll encounter here is the abundance of adorable baby goats!

### Where to Find Them

During spring and early summer, local farms around Piatra Neamț welcome visitors to see their newborn kids (baby goats). These playful creatures are:

- Incredibly energetic and love to jump on everything
- Surprisingly friendly and curious about visitors
- Often found in small herds near the Ceahlău Massif

### Fun Facts About Baby Goats

1. **They can jump within hours of being born** - Baby goats are remarkably agile almost immediately after birth
2. **They're called "kids"** - The term comes from Middle English
3. **They're social animals** - They love playing with their siblings and even other farm animals

# Track some baby goats near Ceahlău
goats = [
